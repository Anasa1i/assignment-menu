Nalli Biryani

Nalli biryani is a rich and flavorful Pakistani rice dish featuring succulent bone marrow (nalli) and tender meat, all slow-cooked with fragrant spices and fluffy basmati rice. Imagine melt-in-your-mouth marrow, aromatic biryani masala, and fluffy grains bursting with flavor - a true culinary delight!
