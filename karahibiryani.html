karahi Biryani

Karahi is a spicy Pakistani dish made with tender lamb or chicken slow-cooked in a karahi pot (a wok-like pan) with yogurt, tomatoes, and chilies. It's known for its intense flavor and melt-in-your-mouth meat, often served with naan bread for scooping up the delicious gravy.
