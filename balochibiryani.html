Balochi Biryani

Balochi biryani is a fragrant rice dish from the Balochistan region of Pakistan, traditionally cooked in a clay oven (tandoor) for a smoky, earthy aroma. It's layered with tender meat (often lamb or chicken), aromatic spices like cardamom and cloves, and topped with crispy fried onions for a delightful textural contrast.
